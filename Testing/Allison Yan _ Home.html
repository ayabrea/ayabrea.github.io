HealthPRO Pharmaceutical Review Process

Optimization of healthcare procurement services
User Experience, Service Design

HealthPRO is a Canadian pharmaceutical procurement company. They wanted to update their pharmaceutical reviewing software as part of their procurement process. Healthcare Human Factors discovered that we needed to examine the other aspects of the process to improve the overall experience of pharmaceutical purchasing and reviewing.

Environmental Analysis

Conducted structured interviews with previous buyers to understand purchasing and reviewing process • Gathered client's informational material and conducted field work at their offices • Completed literature review to examine the current state of purchasing and ideal conditions • Colleague carried out heuristic analysis on client's software • Synthesized themes from analyses of results

Areas of Opportunity

• Target audience: Client's software design team, pharmaceutical reviewing committee • Areas of opportunity: User unfriendly software, long reviewing process

Outcomes

• Determined potential business decisions and prioritized them • Designed easy-to-read and visually appealing report for the client • Presented results to client in a conference call, supporting my colleagues • Invited by client to present on human factors principles to their stakeholders at seminar Literature review Prioritized issue list Report with prioritized business decisions and rationale
