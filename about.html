I'm Allison Yan, and I am a UX designer based in Toronto, Canada. As a designer with a development background, I seek to understand what makes an experience enjoyable, and to figure out how to support people and their work through design. My favourite questions are "Why?" and "How can we make this better?"

In my spare time, I love discovering new things in my city, from music to food. I am a self-taught web developer and am always learning new techniques in visual and web design. I'm usually not without a small notebook, jotting down my ideas and doodles on a subway ride.

 I am currently seeking full-time opportunities.

I'd love to chat about how we can work together.
Shoot me an e-mail at allison.yan@gmail.com, or via contact form. Thanks for stopping by.
