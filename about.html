I'm Allison Yan, and I am an aspiring UX designer based in Toronto, Canada. I have always been passionate about understanding how people think, and how we are affected by our environments. My favourite questions to ask were, and still are, "Why?" and "How can we make this better?" These days, I seek to understand what makes an experience enjoyable, and to figure out how to support people and their work through design.

In my spare time, I love discovering new things in my city, from music to food. I am a self-taught web developer and am always learning new techniques in visual and web design. I'm usually not without a small notebook, jotting down my ideas and doodles on a subway ride. I will be receiving my Master of Health Informatics from the University of Toronto in June 2015.

 I am currently seeking full-time opportunities.

The best way to reach me is allison.yan@gmail.com, or via contact form. Thanks for stopping by.
