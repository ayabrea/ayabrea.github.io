Auntie's Noodle

A classic take on modern Chinese noodle shop
Illustration, Typography

My partner and I reminisced about our childhoods again and found a common thread: food and family. We imagined a Chinese noodle shop that felt like your aunt's house. Playing with textures and patterns, we created ilustrated restaurant print goods to bring back those memories of Chinese furniture, old wall calendars and letter seals...
